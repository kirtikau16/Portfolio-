About me

My name is Kirti Kaushik, and I am currently pursuing my Master's in Computer Applications (MCA) at the Hindu Institute of Management & Technology in Rohtak. With a Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA) from Adarsh Mahila Mahavidyalaya, I have honed my skills in web development, particularly in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript.

My academic journey has been complemented by practical projects, such as cloning the Spotify website to create a responsive replica and developing a resource website for IT students using Blogger. These projects reflect my commitment to continuous learning and my passion for helping others in the tech community.

Beyond academics, I actively participate in extracurricular activities. I have won the Miss Personality title at my college, participated in cultural fests, and engaged in various speech and debate competitions. These experiences have enhanced my communication skills and fostered a well-rounded personality.

In addition to my technical skills, I enjoy reading books, traveling, and walking, which keep me balanced and inspired. Proficient in both English and Hindi, I am eager to contribute to an organization that values growth, responsibility, and innovation. I am excited about the opportunity to apply my skills and creativity to make a significant impact.
